# some of my builds

_The following projects are team projects and mainly hackathon winning projects. The *Backend* and *Architecture design* is done by me._

### [GHOPay](https://github.com/megabyte0x/GHOPay)
- **A decentralized loyalty point system.** Designed to help users and partners get the best out of their money by rewarding them on every(almost) interaction.
- Utilised [Vaults](https://github.com/megabyte0x/GHOPay/blob/77fe0d6b60cbc0c1a7bae27bb1ac94653d283e6b/contracts/src/MainVault.sol#L15)([EIP-4626](https://eips.ethereum.org/eips/eip-4626)) to provide strategize interest rate and minting of the shares as partner reward points.
- Implemented GHO with [Permit2 batch transactions](https://github.com/megabyte0x/GHOPay/blob/77fe0d6b60cbc0c1a7bae27bb1ac94653d283e6b/contracts/src/MainVault.sol#L117) within the Smart Contracts to provide better UX.
- Used [CCIP](https://github.com/megabyte0x/GHOPay/blob/77fe0d6b60cbc0c1a7bae27bb1ac94653d283e6b/contracts/src/CrossChainNFT.sol#L10) to verify the assets owned by the users across chains.
